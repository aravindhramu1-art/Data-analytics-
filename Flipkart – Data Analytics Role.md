**Flipkart – Data Analytics Role**



Flipkart is a leading Indian e-commerce platform, so analytics work is centered around marketplace operations, sales, and customer behavior.



**Key Responsibilities:**



Analyze customer shopping behavior and trends.

Evaluate seller performance and marketplace metrics.

Optimize product pricing, inventory, and supply chain decisions.

Measure marketing campaign effectiveness and customer segmentation.

Build dashboards to track KPIs across business units.



**Tools \& Skills:**



SQL, Python/R for querying and analysis

Excel for reporting

Tableau/Power BI for dashboards

Understanding e-commerce metrics like conversion rate, GMV, churn



Focus Area: Customer behavior, seller analytics, and operational efficiency in e-commerce.





**Amazon – Data Analytics Role**



Amazon is a global e-commerce and tech company, so analytics covers a wide spectrum: e-commerce, logistics, AWS services, and personalization.



**Key Responsibilities:**



Analyze massive datasets across e-commerce, cloud services, and logistics.

Predict demand, optimize inventory, and improve supply chain efficiency.

Build recommendation systems and personalization models.

Track marketing and operational KPIs for business strategy.

Automate dashboards and reports for various teams.

Advanced roles may involve machine learning and predictive modeling.



**Tools \& Skills:**



SQL, Python, R, and sometimes Scala/Java

Tableau, Amazon QuickSight, or internal visualization tools

Statistics, predictive modeling, and data-driven decision-making

Understanding global e-commerce and logistics metrics



Focus Area: Large-scale data analysis, global operations, and predictive insights using advanced analytics.









**Swiggy – Data Analytics Role**



Swiggy is a leading food delivery and quick-commerce platform, so analytics focuses on real-time logistics, customer behavior, and marketplace efficiency.



**Key Responsibilities:**



Analyze large datasets related to orders, delivery, customers, and restaurant partners.

Optimize delivery operations (routing, time efficiency, rider allocation).

Study customer behavior, retention, and ordering patterns.

Improve restaurant performance and marketplace balance (supply vs demand).

Track operational and business KPIs (delivery time, order success rate, cancellations).

Build dashboards and automate reports for different teams.

Advanced roles may involve demand forecasting, churn prediction, and dynamic pricing models.





**Tools \& Skills:**



SQL, Python/R, Excel

Tableau, Power BI, or internal dashboards

Statistics, A/B testing, and data interpretation

Understanding of logistics, food delivery, and hyperlocal marketplaces
